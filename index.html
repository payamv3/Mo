Do you have an old Smartphone that nobody is using? Here are your options!

Resell:

Recent smartphones retain value if in working condition. You could earn some cash by selling your phone. Try the following websites so you can get an estimate of your smartphone's current worth:

    • BackMarket - Online marketplace for refurbished electronics
    • Gazelle - Quick cash for your old devices

    Donate:

    You phone may not fetch a high resell value, but if it is still working and holding a charge, donating it will give it a new life. You can try donating your device, for example:

    • Goodwill - Accepts working electronics at all locations
    • Salvation Army - Electronics donation accepted
